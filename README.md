# Navox Labs

The hiring system is broken by design.

AI screening tools reject 99% of cold applications before any human sees them.
27.4% of all job postings are ghost jobs — positions companies have no intention of filling.
The average job seeker now needs 400–750 applications to receive a single offer.

And the channel that actually works — referrals — is invisible to the people who need it most.

We're researching this system and building tools to fight back.

---

## The research

Everything we build starts here.

**[The AI Recruitment Crisis](https://medium.com/@nahrin_40746/the-ai-hiring-crisis-why-you-need-750-applications-to-get-one-job-2025-data-2fa29b854cf4)** — Algorithmic bias, ghost jobs, and the broken job market. How AI hiring systems institutionalized discrimination at scale, and why cost-per-hire went *up* after AI adoption, not down.

**The Invisible Network** — Graph theory, structural disconnection, and the hidden referral economy. Why new graduates, immigrants, refugees, and career changers are not starting from zero — they're nodes in a graph they've never been given a map to read.

> *"One referral equals 40 cold applications. The infrastructure to access the referral economy already exists. What's missing is the map."*

## Philosophy

Most AI tools ask you to move your work into their environment.  
We think it should be the other way around.

Everything we ship runs locally, stores nothing on our servers,  
and integrates with tools you already use.

Your data is yours. Your code is yours. Your network is yours.
